What are cookies?

Cookies are a baked or cooked treat usually made out of flour, sugar and eggs. You can basically put any kind of toppings within the cookie!

When were cookies first made?

Cookies have been made from as early as 7th Century AD, but didn't come to the US until the 1600's.
